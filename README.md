> A hybrid smart parking system that harvests energy from solar PV panels and piezoelectric ramp sensors, coordinated by an Arduino Mega 2560 controller with real-time slot detection and a live dashboard display.

---

## 🌱 Project Overview

**ECOPark LOT** is a graduation project developed at **El Sewedy University of Technology (SUT)** as part of **Final Project II (2024)**, supervised by **Dr. Mariam Ahmed Sameh**.

The system integrates two renewable energy sources with intelligent parking management to deliver a fully self-powered, sustainable parking facility designed for smart city environments.

## ⚙️ How It Works
---

## 🔋 Energy Sources

| Source | Method | Output |
|--------|--------|--------|
| ☀️ Solar PV Panels | Mounted above parking spaces, harvest sunlight | DC Electricity |
| 🔩 Piezoelectric Sensors | Embedded in ramps, convert vehicle pressure | DC Electricity |

---

## 🛠️ Hardware Components

- **Arduino Mega 2560** — Central microcontroller
- **Solar PV Panels** — Rooftop energy harvesting
- **Piezoelectric Sensors** — Ramp pressure-to-energy conversion
- **Battery System** — Energy storage unit
- **Power Management Unit** — Voltage regulation & load balancing
- **Occupancy Sensors** — Real-time slot detection
- **LCD/LED Dashboard** — Live parking availability display
